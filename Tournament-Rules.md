### Tournament Rules
1. Teams are awarded 3 points for winning a game and 1 point for a draw

### What are the tiebreakers for the group stage?
1. Group points
1. Group goal difference
1. Group goals scored
1. Head-to-head record
1. Fair play record

### How will the best third-place teams be determined?
The third-place teams in the group stage will be ranked by these criteria, in order:
1. Points
2. Goal difference
3. Goals scored
4. Team conduct score
5. FIFA world ranking
